Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Horizon Digital ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our website visitors and clients. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you visit horizondigital.agency.

1. Data Controller

Horizon Digital is the data controller responsible for your personal data. Contact: privacy@horizondigital.agency

2. Data We Collect

We may collect the following data:

  • Contact information: name, email, phone number, company name (when you fill out forms)
  • Website usage data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent (via analytics)
  • Communication data: content of emails and messages you send us

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide requested services
  • To send you marketing communications (only with your consent)
  • To improve our website and services
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Legal Basis (GDPR)

We process your data based on: (a) your consent, (b) performance of a contract, (c) our legitimate interests (improving services, marketing), and (d) legal obligations.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with trusted service providers (hosting, analytics, email) who process data on our behalf under strict agreements.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described, or as required by law. Contact form submissions are retained for up to 24 months.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Object to processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, email privacy@horizondigital.agency.

8. Cookies

See our Cookie Policy for details on how we use cookies.

9. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption, access controls, and regular security assessments.

10. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.

11. Contact

For any privacy-related questions: privacy@horizondigital.agency
